Caprese Salad

The Best Caprese Salad

"This classic Italian caprese salad is quintessential summer fare. Serve with a chilled white wine and enjoy in the sunshine or on a balmy summer evening!"

  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Total: 10 mins
  • Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 3 large heirloom tomatoes, sliced
  • 1(16 ounce) package fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 3 tablesooons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste

Step

  1. Arrange tomato slices, mozzarella slices, and basil leaves on a serving plate, alternating between them. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper.
