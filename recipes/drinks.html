Homemade Italian Cream Soda

"Homemade Italian cream soda, just like you get at the Italian restaurants! You can use any flavored drink syrup but I personally prefer raspberry."

  • Prep: 5mins
  • Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces club soda
  • 4 ounces heavy cream
  • 4 fluid ounces raspberry syrup
  • 2 ice cubes or as needed

Step

  1. Stir together club soda, heavy cream, and raspberry syrup in a pitcher. Fill 2 tall glasses with ice and pour cream mixture on top.
